    How learnbydoing works ?

    “ Our main objective with learn by doing is to create a like minded self learning community.​The reason for this is simple enough.We humans are natural learners. We learn better from the things that we do. So we want to create a platform where students under the guidance of expert industry professionals can learn to work on in-demand industrial technologies by working on projects similar to those in the industries.​ ”

    Our Mission

    Join your preferred telegram group according to the technologies you want to learn.

    Our Plan

    Task and timeline to complete the task will be given on the telegram group itself.

    Our Vision

    Ask our experts if you have doubt in the task in our intractive telegram group.

    Our Result

    The top 5 performers will also receive the internship and placement opportunities.
